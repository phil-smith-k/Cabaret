Contribute a Performance

Share your talent with the Goucher M.A.A.A. community at Cabaret!

For Virtual Cabaret, we will be accepting brief video submissions in a variety of styles – musical performance, monologues, spoken word/poetry, dance etc. We hope to have as much representation from all M.A.A.A. classes as possible!

Video Requirements

  • No more than 3-4 minutes in length
  • .mp4 file type
  • Wide-screen videos are preferable, but not required

To Submit A Video

Please click Submit a Video to be taken to a google form where you can upload your video.

Due to limited time, videos will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.

Tips For Submitting a Great Video

  • Use an external microphone (like the microphone on most Apple headphones)
  • Use your phone, a camera, or you can even record yourself in Zoom.
  • If you want to get extra fancy, you can use a free software or app like iMovie, Windows Movie Maker, or A Cappella to add effects, titles, or audio tracks.

Breakout Rooms

This year’s cabaret will feature break out rooms: a fun new way to connect through 5-minute presentations around the arts that encourage participation.

We are looking for idea submissions for break out room presentations you’d like to see or lead. Please click Submit an Idea to share an idea or sign up to present.

Sample Ideas

  • Dance stretch breaks
  • Cabaret-themed drink making
  • Performing or visual art tutorials
  • Community poem workshop
  • Any other fun arts-related topic that could be covered in 5 minutes

Silent Auction

Join us for an online silent auction to help raise scholarship monies for the M.A.A.A. program. We are looking for silent auction items donations!

Past items have included artwork, gift baskets, museum & concert tickets. We encourage creative auction items that can be used in a socially distant context like Zoom lessons on an instrument, paper proofreading, or a virtual tour.

Donors will be responsible for shipping items.

If you would like to donate an item, please click Contribute to complete the form.
